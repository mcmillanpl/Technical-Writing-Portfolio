# Technical Reference Guides

This section features comprehensive technical manuals and rapid-deployment guides for industrial hardware. These samples demonstrate the ability to manage high-density information, maintain regulatory compliance, and translate engineering specifications into actionable field instructions.

---

## PowerCab2 Installation & Operation Manual
A definitive, 40+ page technical resource for factory-packaged non-stop DC power systems. This manual serves as the primary authority for the product's entire lifecycle.

* **Target Audience**: Electrical Engineers, Facility Managers, and Certified Electricians.
* **Compliance**: Structured to meet **NFPA 70**, **CSA C22.1**, and **UL/CE** safety and installation standards.
* **Key Content**: Theory of operation, detailed mounting procedures, wiring schematics, and long-term maintenance protocols.
* **Focus**: High-stakes safety communication and complex system configuration.
* **[View Technical Manual](./101343_PowerCab2_Manual.pdf)**

---

## SuperTorque 8ZR Quick-Start Guide
A condensed, visual-first deployment guide designed to bridge the gap between unboxing and operation for field technicians.

* **Target Audience**: Field Service Technicians and Installation Contractors.
* **Key Content**: Annotated engineering drawings, floor mounting templates, and simplified AC/DC terminal connection maps.
* **Focus**: Accelerating "Time-to-Power" by extracting critical task-based info from the primary technical manual.
* **Technical Detail**: Includes specific torque requirements and USB-based utility configuration steps.
* **[View Quick-Start Guide](./101346_8R_Quick-Start.pdf)**

## Documentation Standards
Both documents in this section adhere to strict engineering document control standards, including:
* **DCN (Document Change Notice)** tracking for version history.
* **Safety Signaling**: Consistent use of ANSI/ISO-compliant warning and caution symbology.
* **Hardware Integration**: Accurate representation of CAD-based illustrations and physical dimensioning.
